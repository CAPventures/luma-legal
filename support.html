Common questions

  • How do I connect my lights? Go to Settings > Devices and tap Add Device.
  • How do I set up the penalty feature? Settings > Accountability > Set Goal & Amount.
  • How do I cancel a penalty charge? Contact support within 24 hours of the charge.
