Luma Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Luma is a personal sleep, wake, and accountability app by Alex Parker. We take your privacy seriously.

Data we collect

Locally stored: your checklists, flows, device configurations, goal history, and streak data. This is stored on your device only.

Sent to third parties only when you explicitly configure an integration:

  • Philips Hue: local network only, no data leaves your home network
  • Govee, Hatch, Sonos, Spotify, Apple Music: only if connected, only the data needed to control devices
  • Stripe: if you enable the penalty feature, your payment method is stored with Stripe. Luma does not see or store your card details.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We have no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no behavioral tracking
  • We do not sell data to anyone
  • We do not share your goal history, sleep data, or usage patterns with anyone

Push notifications

Used only for routine reminders and goal check-ins you configure.
